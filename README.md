# Ahmedabad Digital Twin

A comprehensive digital twin of **Ahmedabad** for urban visualization, simulation, geospatial analysis, and smart city research.

## Overview

This repository provides a digital twin of Ahmedabad, integrating geospatial data, 3D city models, and simulation capabilities to support research, planning, and real-world applications.

The project was initially developed and initiated by **Parth Devariya** and has since grown through contributions from many developers and community members.

## Use Cases

* 🏙️ **Smart City Planning** – Visualize and evaluate urban development projects before implementation.
* 🚦 **Traffic Simulation** – Analyze traffic flow, congestion patterns, and road network performance.
* 🌳 **Environmental Analysis** – Study air quality, heat islands, green cover, and climate impacts.
* 🚨 **Emergency Response Planning** – Simulate disaster scenarios, evacuation routes, and resource deployment.
* 🏗️ **Infrastructure Management** – Monitor and plan utilities, transportation networks, and public assets.
* 📡 **IoT Integration** – Connect real-time sensor data for monitoring and operational insights.
* 🛰️ **Geospatial Analysis** – Perform GIS-based spatial analysis using satellite imagery and city datasets.
* 🤖 **AI & Machine Learning Research** – Develop and evaluate AI models using realistic urban environments.
* 🚗 **Autonomous Vehicle Testing** – Validate perception, navigation, and planning algorithms in a virtual city.
* 🎮 **Simulation & Visualization** – Create immersive 3D experiences for education, demonstrations, and research.
* 📚 **Academic Research** – Support universities and research organizations working on urban computing and digital twins.
* 🏢 **Government & Civic Applications** – Assist policymakers and municipal agencies with data-driven decision making.

## Features

* High-quality digital representation of Ahmedabad
* 3D visualization and interactive exploration
* GIS and geospatial data integration
* Extensible architecture for simulations
* Support for AI, robotics, and smart city applications
* Community-driven development
